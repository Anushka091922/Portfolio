Hello!

I'm an undergraduate student at VJTI Mumbai. Before embarking on my machine learning journey, I explored ways to align my enthusiasm for machine learning research with my entrepreneurial aspirations.

On the research front, I have focused on developing machine learning models that enhance natural language processing (NLP) relevance. Currently, I am building a model under the guidance of Professor Mahesh Shirole, an Associate Professor at VJTI, Mumbai, aiming to improve the relevance of search results for users. This project reflects my commitment to creating algorithms that ensure user-centric outcomes in information retrieval.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have gained practical experience in the startup ecosystem. For instance, during my internship at Infosys, I developed a fake news detection model using ensemble techniques and transformers to enhance information accuracy and combat misinformation in digital media. I also participated in the Myntra WeForShe Hackathon, where I created a fashion search engine that utilized advanced query options and vector embeddings for improved search accuracy.

Through these experiences, I strive to bridge the gap between theoretical research and real-world applications, ensuring that my work contributes positively to both fields.
